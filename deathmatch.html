Deathmatch, a free for all mode, testing your individual skill.

Deathmatch, a game mode favoured mostly by Swords Only players, is a game mode that allows you and your team to see who's better at eliminating eachother.


With the probability of always being at danger, it's a game that'll leave you looking around every corner with care, trying not to get killed in the chaos of combat.


This mode can be won in two different ways; either by having more kills then the other team when the time runs out, or to be the first team to reach 100 kills

A gamemode that can be won either by an all out brawl, or by team driven combat, every match that you play is truly different, both in map choice and in the players playstyle that have joined your, or their team.
