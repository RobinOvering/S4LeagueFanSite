Touchdown, what is the purpose of this mode.

Touchdown is a game mode designed to bring a competitive edge into S4 League. The goal is to get the ball displayed on the upper right image to the goal displayed on the lower left.

The maps are symmetrical, with some exceptions, and everyone starts out in their own base's spawnplace, as depicted by the upper left image.

This game mode is won by either getting 10 touchdowns before the other team, the timer running out and having more touchdowns then the opposite team, or in the case of a tie, by the players having a higher total individual score then the other team.

As a fast-paced, strategical, highly skilled and teamwork reliant gamemode, It gives S4 League it's competitive nature, having you improve everytime you play. By far, one of S4 League's most commonly played game modes, and the go-to skill measurement for competitive players.
