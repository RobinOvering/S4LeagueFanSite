Chaser, a thrilling skill tester, designed to keep you on your toes.

A game mode that pits you against every other player in the room. Every round a random player gets selected to be the Chaser. He or she must then eliminate all the other players in the room. If the Chaser fails to do so, the players that did die get 5 points, and the surviving players get 15 points.


As a Chaser, you have two options, you can either go for anyone and everything, racking up two points per kill, up to a maximum of 25 points, or go for the targets, awarding double points, up to a maximum of 44 points.

At the start of the game, the target is selected at random. The Chaser then gets 3 minutes to try and kill everyone present in the room. After the first round, the target is selected based on the highest score.


Other then killing targets, surviving rounds or killing everyone, you can gain points by damaging the Chaser using your weapons. A bar will display as an indicator to show how many times you have to hit the Chaser to gain 2 points. Though, this can be a difficult task to do, since the Chaser has increased movement speed.
